Read the Docs
###################

|RTD| is a free service that publishes and hosts documentation.

In fact, this syllabus is published through |RTD|.

We will use |RTD| to publish our :ref:`Sphinx <Sphinx>` projects.

* To publish our Sphinx project through |RTD|.
